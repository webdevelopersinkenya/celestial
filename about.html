About Us

Deseret Cruises International was founded in 2024 with a vision to offer exceptional cruise experiences while uplifting lives. Born from a heartfelt conversation between a local Bishop and dedicated members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints working at MSC Cruises, the idea emerged from a deep concern for unemployment and the desire to create meaningful opportunities. Observing neighbors traveling in luxurious cruises across Europe and the United States, the Bishop asked: “Why not us too?”

Guided by faith, compassion, and a commitment to service, our founders combined their expertise in hospitality, finance, and operations to build a company that values safety, comfort, and unforgettable journeys. Deseret Cruises International, our signature cruise line, was born — a company dedicated not only to leisure and adventure but also to creating dignified employment, fostering spiritual enrichment, and connecting communities. From humble beginnings, Deseret Cruises International continues to grow, serving travelers worldwide while inspiring memorable experiences at sea.
